The Process

You’ve already started the movement. We help drive home results. Tell us your story and let’s find ways to fuel your mission.

A Super Quick Overview

  • Apply by filling out the form. If you have questions, contact us at help@drivetocreate.org.
  • We'll review your application and set up a call with you if we feel like you're a good fit.
  • Then, the DTC Board votes to determine how we should proceed. If approved, we schedule a Drive Day. If we do not approve, you can re-apply in 6 months.
  • Once it's time for your Drive Day, we arrive bright and early and spend the next 12 hours creating, educating and changing.
  • After your Drive Day, we'll check in every month to make sure things are going smoothly and to answer any questions you may have. We're always available for advice.
